ABOUT ME

Hey Guys , I'm glad you'd like to know more about me ! My name is Mohammed Arbaaz , a 20 year old Information Science Engineer from Bangalore , India . As relevant as the posts are , you could also guess that i'm a music producer & Dj . That's right i've been into Music & Computer's since at a very young age .

My fascination in Computer Science started back when i was in Grade 8 . I didn't understand programming back then , but my Dad (who loved coding) introduced me to the world of Programming . Ever since then , i haven't turned my back on Computer Science & it was a path of constant learning !

My notice to the exploding EDM scene started back in 2013 - 2014 . It was when i first heard Hardwell - Spaceman & a couple of more such tracks , that i was really into this kind of Music . I was fascinated by the way this kind of music was made & hence i started producing music from 2015. Since then it was just constant hardwork & practice that landed me to the kind of Artist i am now ! Learning Djing was not far away & i soon paved my way to become a Dj as well .

Hope my backstory provided a bit of insight into my life . If you want to further know about , please check out my Technical Resume Below (or) visit my Producer section of Website !
